English  I beg to God  is the lead single from the Spanish studio album Un Día Normal by the Latin music singer and songwriter Juanes  released in 2002 in Spain and Latin America  In 2006  the song was re released in some countries in Europe  right after the success of "La Camisa Negra"  which charted in almost every European country in the top five  It reached number 1 in twelve countries on three different continents  "A Dios le Pido" spent 47 consecutive weeks on the "Hot Latin Tracks" Billboard reaching number 2  kept out from the number one spot by Chayanne's Y Tú Te Vas and Jennifer Peña's El Dolor de Tu Presencia   This track won the Latin Grammy Award for Best Rock Song at the Latin Grammy Awards of 2002  the second year in a row for the singer  The song is an anthem asking God to bless and protect the singer's family  future children  and close friends  It quickly became a hymn for peace through all of Latin America  particularly because of the line "a Dios le pido que mi pueblo no derrame tanta sangre y se levante mi gente "    "I ask God  may my country not shed so much blood and may my people rise up"   Track listings edit  Digital download  Surco September 1  2003
