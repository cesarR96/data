Sessions  2014  Tigo Music Sessions2014          Tigo Music Sessions is the third live album by Colombian recording artist Juanes  The album was released in two commercial music formats  the first was on DVD format that was distributed through Colombian newspaper El Tiempo  in the editions of August 15 and 16 of 2014  1  The second was an exclusive release to commercial music streaming service Deezer  sponsored by Tigo Music released on August 15  2014 by Universal Music Latin  2  The live album served to hold the promotion of his sixth studio album Loco de Amor  3     Contents   1 Track
